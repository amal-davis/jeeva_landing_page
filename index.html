Welcome to Jeeva Ayur Heritage,

Welcome to Jeeva Ayur Heritage, where timeless traditions and holistic wellness converge to create a sanctuary for your mind, body, and soul. Nestled in the heart of Kerala, the cradle of Ayurveda, our heritage retreat offers an immersive journey into the ancient wisdom of natural healing and cultural richness. Here, the serene landscapes, the gentle rhythm of traditional music, and the soothing ambiance of nature provide the perfect backdrop for rejuvenation and relaxation.

At Jeeva Ayur Heritage, we invite you to experience the essence of Ayurveda through personalized treatments, nourishing cuisine, and enriching cultural activities.

WHY CHOOSING US IS A SMART IDEA?

Panchakarma

Panchakarma, a cornerstone of Ayurvedic medicine, is a holistic detoxification and rejuvenation therapy designed to cleanse the body of toxins and restore its natural balance.

Yoga And Meditations

Yoga and meditation are ancient practices rooted in the rich traditions of India. Both are integral parts of a holistic approach to health and well-being, offering profound benefits for the body, mind, and spirit.

Basic Ayurvedha Classes

Basic Ayurveda classes provide an introduction to the foundational principles and practices of this time-tested medical science. These classes are designed for anyone interested in understanding how to apply Ayurvedic wisdom to their daily lives for improved health and well-being.

Consultation With Doctor

This consultation involves a detailed assessment of an individual's physical, mental, and emotional health to determine their unique constitution (Prakriti) and any current imbalances (Vikriti).

OUR PROGRAMS

BODY PURIFICATION

This Ayurveda package cleanses the body and mind. Among other things, the focus is on cleansing procedures (Panchakarma), oil applications (Snehana), herbal steam baths (Swendanam), pouring of warm oils onto the forehead (Shirodhara) and – as a climax – the royal oil bath treatment (Pizhichil). In their treatments, doctors and therapists draw on the effectiveness of traditional remedies – such as Ayurvedic oils, herbs and ghee.

BODY IMMUNISATION

Getting older and still not aging: With the Body Immunization Package we come very close to this wish. The focus is on the knowledge of the Rasayanas. These are primarily rejuvenating remedies. The treatment leads to a transformation of the body tissue (dhatus) and counteracts negative influences.

WEIGHTLOSS

Losing weight, feeling freer and more comfortable as well as prevent the harmful effects that can occur due to overweight – these are the goals of this special Ayurvedic treatment. Traditional medicinal herbs play a central role in this treatment: they unfold their effect in powder massages, oils, steam baths and medicines. Individual and varied nutrition supports the treatment.

REJUVENATION

This package is designed to relax and vitalize the body. In addition to medical steam baths, herbal medicine and soothing face masks, it also includes two highlights of Ayurvedic treatment: full-body massages (Abhyanga) and pouring of warm liquids onto the forehead (Shirodhara). Their base are warm herbal oils, which are mixed according to centuries-old recipes.

STRESS MANAGEMENT PACKAGE

If you suffer from stress or burn-out symptoms, the Stress Management Package is the right choice. Among other things, this package includes pouring of warm oil onto the forehead (Shirodhara), herbal steam baths as well as yoga and meditation. It is the goal to let body and soul come to rest.

BEAUTY CARE PACKAGE

The success of these treatments is obvious: your complexion is visibly more radiant, your skin smoother, and your body firmer. The Beauty Care Package delivers on what it promises: it provides your skin with protection and beauty. This treatment pursues one goal above all else: you are to feel completely relaxed.
